2017 DSE Paper I Q19

In triangle ABC, $BC = 24\text{ cm}$, $\angle BAC = 30^\circ$ and $\angle ACB = 42^\circ$.
Note: You are viewing the 2D layout.
Part (a) Find the length of $AC$.

Find $\angle ABC$: $180^\circ - 30^\circ - 42^\circ = 108^\circ$

Using Sine Rule: $\frac{AC}{\sin 108^\circ} = \frac{24}{\sin 30^\circ}$

$AC = \frac{24 \sin 108^\circ}{\sin 30^\circ} \approx 45.7 \text{ cm}$

Part (b) The metal sheet $PQRS$ is now folded along $QS$. It is given that the angle between plane $PQS$ and plane $QRS$ is $80^\circ$.
Note: You are viewing the 2D layout.
(b)(i) Find the distance between $C$ and $F$.

Using similar triangles $\triangle ADF \sim \triangle CEF$: $\frac{CF}{CE} = \frac{AF}{AD}$

$\frac{CF}{2} = \frac{CF + 45.65}{10}$

$8CF = 91.3 \implies CF \approx 11.4 \text{ cm}$

(b)(ii) Find area of $\triangle ABC$.

First, find $AB$ using the Sine Rule in $\triangle ABC$:
$\frac{AB}{\sin 42^\circ} = \frac{24}{\sin 30^\circ} \implies AB \approx 32.118\text{ cm}$

Calculate $AF$:
$AF = AC + CF = 45.651 + 11.413 = 57.064\text{ cm}$

Area of $\triangle ABF = \frac{1}{2}(AB)(AF)\sin(\angle BAF)$:
$\text{Area} = \frac{1}{2}(32.118)(57.064)\sin 30^\circ$

Area of $\triangle ABF \approx 458.2 \text{ cm}^2$

(b)(iii) Find inclination of $ABC$ to the horizontal ground.

The inclination $\theta$ is the angle between plane $ABC$ and the ground. Since $B$ and $F$ are on the ground, $BF$ is the intersection line.

Find $BF$ using Cosine Rule in $\triangle ABF$:
$BF^2 = 32.118^2 + 57.064^2 - 2(32.118)(57.064)\cos 30^\circ \implies BF \approx 33.364\text{ cm}$

Let $h$ be the height of $\triangle ABF$ from $A$ to $BF$:
$h = \frac{2 \times \text{Area}}{BF} = \frac{2 \times 458.18}{33.364} \approx 27.466\text{ cm}$

$\sin \theta = \frac{AD}{h} = \frac{10}{27.466}$

$\theta \approx 21.4^\circ$

(b)(iv) Claim: the area of $BDF$ is greater than $460\text{ cm}^2$. Do you agree?.

Calculate the sides of $\triangle BDF$ on the ground:
$DF = \sqrt{AF^2 - AD^2} \approx 56.181\text{ cm}$
$BD = \sqrt{AB^2 - AD^2} \approx 30.522\text{ cm}$

Find $\angle BDF$ using Cosine Rule:
$\cos \angle BDF = \frac{BD^2 + DF^2 - BF^2}{2(BD)(DF)} \approx 0.8674$

$\text{Area of } \triangle BDF = \frac{1}{2}(BD)(DF)\sin(\angle BDF)$:
$\text{Area} \approx \frac{1}{2}(30.522)(56.181)(0.4975) \approx 426.5 \text{ cm}^2$

Since $426.5 < 460$, the claim is incorrect. I do not agree.
