2024 DSE Paper I Q18

Question Summary 1/2 (UpdateForNewExercise) Delete this before released.

PQRS a quadrilateral, where $PQ = 12\text{ cm}$, $PS = 10\text{ cm}$, $QR = 13\text{ cm}$, $\angle QPS = 82^\circ$ and $\angle QRS = 65^\circ$.

Solution 1/4 (UpdateForNewExercise) Delete this before released.

(a)(i) Find the length of $QS$.

$QS = \sqrt{12^2 + 10^2 - 2(12)(10)\cos(82^\circ)} \approx 14.5126 \text{ cm}$

Solution 2/4 (UpdateForNewExercise) Delete this before released.

(a)(ii) Find $\angle RQS$.

$\frac{13}{\sin(\angle RSQ)} = \frac{14.5126}{\sin(65^\circ)} \implies \angle RQS \approx 60.7^\circ$
