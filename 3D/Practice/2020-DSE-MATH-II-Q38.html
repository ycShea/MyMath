DSE 2020 Math II Q38

Instruction: In the figure, $ABCDEF$ is a right triangular prism. $P$ is a point on $AE$. If $AB=AC=12$, $AP=9$, $EP=5$ and $BD=2k$, find the area of $\Delta BDP$.

🖱️ Drag to rotate | 🔍 Scroll to zoom

1. Height of Prism: $AE = 9 + 5 = 14$.
2. Side $DP$: In $\Delta DEP$, $DP = \sqrt{12^2 + 5^2} = 13$.
3. Side $BP$: In $\Delta BAP$, $BP = \sqrt{12^2 + 9^2} = 15$.
4. Side $BD$: Given as $2k$.
